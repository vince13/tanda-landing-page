Strategic Capital – Equity Terms

Overview for prospective partners. Subject to legal review.

Structure

We offer equity in the range of ~10% to ~20%, structured around capital invested, time commitment, and strategic contribution.

~10% – Capital only

For partners contributing primarily financial investment (e.g. $120K). Passive or light-touch involvement.

~20% – Capital + active participation

For partners who invest capital and commit significant time (e.g. 20+ hrs/week): strategy, growth, partnerships, funding preparation. Co-builder, not advisor.

Vesting

Equity for the ~20% tier is subject to vesting (typically 24-36 months) to align long-term commitment. Structure to be finalized with legal counsel.

Process

Share your interest via the Strategic Partners form. We screen for fit, then schedule a call to discuss terms in detail.
