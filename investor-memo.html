Tanda Venture Studio — Investor Memo

Confidential · March 2026

Summary

Tanda is a venture studio building secure infrastructure and economic rails for the AI-native generation. We operate a vertically integrated stack: ForgeSpace (secure agent runtime), OpenCareer / ResumeAI (career ecosystem), and Tanda Commerce (AI-native video commerce). We lead with traction (ResumeAI: 10,000+ resumes) and invest in moat (ForgeSpace).

Thesis

AI is restructuring work, value creation, and commerce—but tools alone are not enough. Execution without distribution fails. Tools without community plateau. Commerce without infrastructure fragments. We build ventures that reinforce one another technically and economically.

Products

  • ForgeSpace — Secure, sandboxed runtime for AI agents. Zero-trust: agents run locally without accessing users' unauthorized data. Live at forge.tanda.media.
  • OpenCareer / ResumeAI — Career support ecosystem. ResumeAI: ATS optimization, job-specific resumes. Global focus (UAE, US, EU). 10,000+ resumes generated. Pro ($20/mo).
  • Tanda Commerce — AI-native video commerce. Product tagging, affiliate, creator monetization. 16,000+ waitlist. Production-ready at app.tanda.media.

Market

TAM/SAM/SOM: Tanda Commerce $305B / $50B / $5B (Africa + SE Asia + LATAM). ForgeSpace $139B / $11B / $1B (agent infrastructure). OpenCareer $35B / $4B / $200M (recruitment & career tools).

Business Model

Revenue GTM: OpenCareer/ResumeAI — B2C subscriptions. Infrastructure GTM: ForgeSpace — B2B runtime. Capital allocation: lead with ResumeAI traction, invest in ForgeSpace moat, optionality via Tanda Commerce.

Strategic Capital

We collaborate with partners who invest long-term, contribute strategically, and understand infrastructure. Use of funds: scale ResumeAI (global), strengthen ForgeSpace, accelerate adoption. Equity structure: ~10% (capital only) to ~20% (capital + active participation) with vesting.
